Output a single-line JSON of up to 10 branding and advertising keywords and frequency from the page.

Keywords should have the following qualities:
* has to be an adjective
* one word
* engaging and unique (have high text burstiness)
* compelling enough to include in a high-quality ad

Keywords should not have the following qualities:
* is not an adjective
* should not be part of or be the company name (e.g., if company is Red Bull, "red" and "bull" should be excluded)
* specific locations
* boring business terms (e.g. sales, brand, investment, growth)
* people names

If term isn't in English, translate to English.

JSON should follow the schema [{"keyword" => str, "frequency" => int}]. Normalize keywords all to lowercase
