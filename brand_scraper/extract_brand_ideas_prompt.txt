Output a single-line JSON of up to 10 branding and advertising ideas from the page.

Ideas should have the following qualities:
* engaging and unique (have high text burstiness)
* compelling enough to include in a high-quality ad
* quotes (if too long, shorten to become slogans)
* keywords
* high quality slogans

Ideas should not have the following qualities:
* specific locations
* boring business terms (e.g. sales, brand, investment, growth)
* people names

If term isn't in English, include the English translation in parentheses.

JSON should follow the schema [{"branding_idea" => str, "ad_potential" => 0.0-1.0, "frequency" => int}]. Normalize keywords all to lowercase.
